Digital Millennium Copyright Act Policy

As this site is an educational project and does not host any files, our DMCA policy is straightforward. We do not store copyrighted material on our servers. Our service functions as an index, providing metadata that is generated by users for demonstration purposes.

If you are a copyright holder and believe that a link on our site points to infringing material, please note that removing the link from our index will not remove the content from the internet. The content exists on the computers of the peers in the BitTorrent network.

However, as part of our educational demonstration, we will comply with valid takedown requests for links in our index. To file a notice, please provide the following information to the contact email:

  • The specific magnet link(s) you wish to have removed.
  • Proof of your identity as the copyright holder or authorized agent.
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized.
